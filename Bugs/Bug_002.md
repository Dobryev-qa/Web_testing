## Summary
Desktop category displays products from other categories.

---

## Project Information
- Project: WebShop Demo OpenCart
- URL: https://demo.opencart.com/en-gb/catalog/desktops
- Component: Catalog – Desktop Category
- Platform: Web

---

## Environment
- OS: macOS Tahoe 26.0.1
- Browser: Google Chrome 142.0.7444.60

---

## Reporter
Dmytro Dobryev

## Date
25.10.2025

## Preconditions
- Homepage is opened

---

## Steps to Reproduce
1. Navigate to the **Desktop** category
2. Observe the list of displayed products

---

## Expected Result
- Only products belonging to the **Desktop** category are displayed  
- Products from other categories (e.g. Laptops, Tablets, Phones) are not shown

---

## Actual Result
- Products from multiple categories are displayed on the Desktop category page
