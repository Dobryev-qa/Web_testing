# Bug Report – Bug_001

## Summary
Homepage main slider redirects to incorrect product and contains non-clickable banner.

---

## Project Information
- Project: WebShop Demo OpenCart
- URL: https://demo.opencart.com/en-gb?route=common/home
- Component: Homepage – Main Slider
- Platform: Web

---

## Environment
- OS: macOS Tahoe 26.0.1
- Browser: Google Chrome 142.0.7444.60

---

## Reporter
Dmytro Dobryev

## Date
25.10.2025

---

## Preconditions
- Homepage is opened

---

## Steps to Reproduce
1. Open the homepage
2. Click the first banner (iPhone image)
3. Observe the redirection
4. Click the second banner (MacBook image)

---

## Expected Result
- Each banner redirects to the correct product or product category page
- All banners are clickable

---

## Actual Result
- First banner redirects to the **Tablets** category instead of the iPhone product/category
- Second banner is not clickable

---

## Severity
Major

## Priority
High

---

## Notes
- First banner contains an incorrect `href` value
- Second banner is missing the `href` attribute
